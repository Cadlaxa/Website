About Her

Vhalerie Canete Ramos

Her name is Vhalerie Canete Ramos who is known by the nickname Vhal, was born on November 23, 2004 at San Juan De Dios Hospital in Pasay City, Manila. She then first Graduated her elementary years at Imus Pilot Elementary School in the Year 2016-2017 with the title of Valedictorian. Then she finished her Junior Highschool and Senior Highschool at Del Pilar Academy claiming the spot of with Highest Honor of her batch. During her College Years she chose Cavite State University Imus Campus as her number 1 University choice and luckily she passed the entrance exam and took on the course of Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Graduated as one of the Magna Cumlaude of their batch. She is now one of the famous Business Tycoon in the Philippines and is the CEO of iPineapple Company, She is now married with Kentaro Maeda and is now living happily with their 2 children in Sendai City, Japan.
